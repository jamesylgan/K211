Overview

Academies

 Communication Arts Academy

 The Communication Arts Academy is one of three mini-schools within a larger middle school environment. We are a community of learners whose focus centers on communication arts including creative writing, journalism, telecommunications and playwriting. While we have a rigorous curriculum in all major subjects, our academy stresses English Language Arts and Social Studies. Students communicate via a student council set up to emulate our country’s governing system. We believe in strong student involvement.

Cultural Arts Academy

 The Cultural Arts Academy is comprised of the following: drama, music, creative arts, poetry, computerized graphic design and dance. Literacy is infused through the arts. Students in the Cultural Arts Academy understand that literacy is one of the keys to success. Students are empowered, enabled, engaged and enlightened through our focused program. Students are videotaped and their works are shared in the community.

Special Programs

 ASTRAL Gifted &Talented Program

 The core philosophy of the gifted and talented program is the SEM model. The basic objective is to provide enrichment to all the students in diverse subject areas. Students will receive additional instruction in academics (advance classes such as Math A Prep, Spanish Proficiency Regents, Advanced Literature and Earth Science Regents), Technology, Foreign Language and the Arts. Through the Acceleration Process, our Gifted and Talented students will be more prepared for the challenges that arise in High School and College.
